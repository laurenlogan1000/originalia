How your art is protected

Licensing your work
isn't the same as losing it.

◆

You own it. Always.

Licensing is not selling. Your IP never transfers to a buyer. Every agreement grants use rights only — your characters remain yours.

◆

Exclusivity is enforced.

If you list a character as exclusive, that means exclusive. We manage the agreements and pursue violations on your behalf — you don't have to chase anyone.

◆

Full transaction visibility.

You see every license issued — who bought it, what use type, what they paid, and when. No black box.

◆

You approve every deal.

Nothing goes out without your sign-off. You review the buyer and terms before any license is issued.

On AI training use

AI companies need original characters. Right now, most artists never see a dollar from it — the work gets taken, not licensed. We're building AI training as an opt-in licensing category so that if you want to participate, you get paid for it. And if you don't, you have real backing behind that choice. We're working with legal and nonprofit partners already fighting for artists in this space. Our goal isn't a defensive crouch — it's a world where original art has more value, not less.
